Skidd McMarxx

renowned professional hoverboard champion of Rilgar

Alternatively spelled: Skidd McMarx, Skid McMarx

Games that Skidd McMarxx has appeared:

  • Ratchet & Clank(2002) - He is a renowned professional hoverboard champion of Rilgar,
  • Ratchet & Clank: Up Your Arsenal(2004) - Has joined the Q-Force, His codename for the team was "Shadow Dude".
  • Ratchet & Clank(2016) remake - He was the nephew of Agnogg Buckwash and owned a McMarx Sports Shack on Aridia.
  • Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart(2021) - he is in Megalopolis alongside Captain Qwark and Rusty Pete as they participate in the Festival of Heroes as hosts.
